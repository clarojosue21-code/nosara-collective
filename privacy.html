Section 1

Who We Are

Nosara Collective Conscience ("we", "us", "our") is a vacation rental booking platform operating at nosaracollectiveconscience.com, based in Nosara, Guanacaste, Costa Rica. We connect guests with premium vacation properties in the Nosara area while directing 5% of every booking to local community and conservation projects.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information when you use our website or make a reservation.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use it for advertising. We collect only what is necessary to process your reservation and communicate with you about your stay.

Section 2

What Data We Collect

When You Make a Reservation

  • Full name — to identify the primary guest
  • Email address — to send booking confirmation and communications
  • Phone number (WhatsApp) — optional, for pre-arrival coordination
  • Country of residence — optional, for reporting and experience personalization
  • Travel dates and number of guests — to process your reservation
  • Selected property and services — to fulfill your booking
  • Payment method — we record which method you selected (PayPal or Wise), but we do not store card numbers or bank details

Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address — collected by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and abuse prevention
  • Browser and device type — for basic analytics and compatibility
  • Pages visited and time on site — if analytics tools are enabled

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Credit card numbers or bank account details (handled entirely by PayPal or Wise)
  • Passport or ID numbers
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Data from social media accounts
Section 3

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Reservation processing — creating and managing your booking in our system
  • Confirmation emails — sending your booking details, reference number and payment summary
  • Pre-arrival coordination — communicating check-in details, house instructions, and concierge services
  • Customer support — responding to modifications, cancellations or questions
  • Legal compliance — maintaining transaction records as required by Costa Rican tax law
  • Platform improvement — understanding how guests use our site to improve the experience

We will never use your email address to send unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent. If you wish to receive updates about new properties or special offers, we will always ask for your opt-in separately.

Section 4

Third-Party Service Providers

To operate our platform, we work with the following trusted providers. Each receives only the minimum data necessary to perform their function:

Supabase
Database · supabase.com

Stores reservation records securely. All data is encrypted at rest. Servers located in the United States.

Resend
Email delivery · resend.com

Sends booking confirmation emails. Your email address is passed to Resend solely to deliver transactional messages.

PayPal
Payment processing · paypal.com

Processes credit/debit card payments. We never see or store your card details — PayPal handles all payment data under their own Privacy Policy.

Wise
Bank transfer · wise.com

Facilitates international bank transfers. Wise processes transfers independently under their own Privacy Policy.

Netlify
Hosting · netlify.com

Hosts our website and serverless functions. May collect IP addresses and access logs for security purposes.

Cloudinary
Image hosting · cloudinary.com

Serves property photos. No personal data is shared with Cloudinary.

None of our service providers are authorized to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

Section 5

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

  • Active reservations: for the duration of your stay plus 5 years, as required by Costa Rican tax and commercial law
  • Cancelled reservations: 2 years from the date of cancellation, for dispute resolution purposes
  • Email communications: 2 years
  • Site analytics: 13 months (rolling)

After the applicable retention period, your data is permanently deleted from our systems. You may request earlier deletion — see Your Rights below.

Section 6

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations
  • Portability: request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to any processing of your data beyond what is strictly necessary for your reservation

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@nosaracollectiveconscience.com. We will respond within 30 days.

These rights are consistent with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for guests located in the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents, and Costa Rica's Law on Protection of the Person against the Processing of their Personal Data (Law No. 8968).

Section 7

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies strictly necessary for functionality:

  • Session cookies: temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain your cart and booking state
  • Analytics cookies: if Google Analytics is enabled, these track anonymous usage patterns to help us improve the site. You may opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site cookies of any kind. We do not participate in behavioral advertising networks.
