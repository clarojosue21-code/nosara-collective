◈ Our Mission

As tourism continues to grow, much of the revenue generated here leaves the region through large international booking platforms. Nosara Collective Conscience was created to change that.

We're building a locally driven network of homes, experiences, and services — created with homeowners, local businesses, and conscious travelers who believe tourism should strengthen the places it touches, not extract from them.
