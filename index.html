◈ Our Mission

As tourism continues to grow, much of the revenue generated here leaves the region through large international booking platforms. Nosara Collective Conscience was created to change that.

We are building a locally driven network of homes, experiences, and services that keeps more tourism dollars circulating within the community. Together with homeowners, local businesses, and conscious travelers, we are creating a model where tourism directly contributes to the people, culture, and future of Nosara.

Every reservation becomes an opportunity to support local families, strengthen community initiatives, and preserve the spirit that makes Nosara one of the world's most special coastal destinations.

Travel with purpose. Stay with impact. Surf good waves everyday 🌴✨
